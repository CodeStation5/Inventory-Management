About us at Maple Syrup Computing


Are you looking for a custom built PC? You've come to the right place! Maple Syrup Computing is your 1 stop shop to buy PC parts and build yourself a PC!

We specialize in creating custom built computers, from low-end computers to mining rigs. All tailored to your needs. Whether you're a gamer, a professional in need of powerful workstations, or someone looking for a reliable computer for everyday use, we've got you covered. At Maple Syrup Computing, we're passionate about technology and dedicated to ensuring that our customers get the best products and support.
