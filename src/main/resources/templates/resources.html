Computer Information


Need help deciding on parts?

RAM:----Random access memory. It's the part of the computer that stores data that's currently being used. 8gb is recommended for weaker PC's, 64gb for powerful ones.

CPU:----Central processing unit. It's the part of the computer that does all of the processing work.

Case:----Computer Case. Holds all of your PC's parts.

Hard Drive:----Stores all of your important files for safe keeping.

GPU:----Graphics processing unit. Generates graphics for the computer, generally whatever you see on your monitor.
